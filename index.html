About the Artist

Jane Doe is an artist specializing in black and white ink drawings of architecture and landscapes. Her work is characterized by intricate line work and a keen eye for detail, often capturing the essence of a place through its structural form. Inspired by architectural blueprints and classic etchings, her style blends precision with artistic expression.

With a background in design, Jane brings a unique perspective to her art, focusing on the interplay of light, shadow, and texture to create depth and dimension. Her pieces invite viewers to explore the world through a monochromatic lens, appreciating the beauty of form and composition.
