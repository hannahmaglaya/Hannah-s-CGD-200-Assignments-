About Me

I'm Hannah, and I started doing ink drawings when I had COVID thrice during the pandemic. I studied Ink Drawiwing. I'm lazy, so I don't use a pencil. I did Inktober back in 2022. I drew according to their criteria. I'm really interested in architectural stuff and how buildings look, so you'll see a lot of that in my drawings.
