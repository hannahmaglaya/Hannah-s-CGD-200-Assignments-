About Me

I'm Hannah, and I started doing ink drawings when I got super bored with COVID during the pandemic. I just grabbed some ink and paper, and it became a thing! I even did Inktober back in 2022. I'm really interested in architectural stuff and how buildings look, so you'll see a lot of that in my drawings.
